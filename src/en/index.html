Who am I?

I hold over 10 years of experience developping web sites and APISs. I've worked for organizations of all size, helping them establish their presence online.

As a programmer, I want to put that experience to use collaborating with community orgs in Montréal/Tio'tia:ke. I'm prioritizing projects that revolve around the city's ballroom, burelesque and drag scenes, along with community-care projects, and queer, feminist, disabled and neurodivergent spaces.

I want to help us build the tools we need to march toward social justice, respond to the climate emergency, and promote active/public transit, public health and access to affordable housing.
